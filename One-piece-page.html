One Piece

“One Piece” is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda1. The story is set in a world dominated
by pirates and follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy who accidentally eats the Gomu Gomu no Mi, a
magical Devil Fruit that turns his body into rubber2. Inspired by his transformation and a pirate crew known as the
Shanks, Luffy vows to become the Pirate King2.
